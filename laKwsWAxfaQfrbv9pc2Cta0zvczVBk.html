7 products for good vision

Essential nutrients, such as carotenoids, vitamins A and C, omega-3 fatty acids (o-3A), minerals, and antioxidants, play a key role in good vision.

It is useful for the preservation of vision add these 7 foods to your diet.

1. Blueberries.

If you want to have good eyesight, you should regularly eat blueberries. It is rich in many vitamins and antioxidants, has anti-inflammatory and strengthening vascular action.

High concentration of antioxidants in blueberries reduces the risk of macular degeneration (macular degeneration), cataracts, and other eye diseases.

2. Carrots.

Carrots are certainly one of the most beneficial foods for eye health. It is an excellent source of beta-carotene, from which the body produces vitamin A. Vitamin A helps prevent night blindness (hemeralopia).

3. Salmon.

Salmon, like other oily fish (mackerel, herring, sardines, tuna), is rich in O-3s, essential for good eye health. O-3 LCDS play an important role in normal retinal function, helping to prevent and treat dry eye syndrome.

Still in the salmon still a lot vitamin D, which is also good for vision.

4. Green leafy vegetables.

Spinach and other leafy vegetables are rich in zeaxanthin and lutein , which are carotenoids found in the macula of the retina. These yellow pigments absorb UV light, which is particularly harmful to the retina.

Row studies have shown that zeaxanthin and lutein reduce the risk of chronic eye diseases such as cataracts and age-related macular DEGENERATION.

5. Olive oil.

The main reason why olive oil is good for the eyes is that it helps the body better absorb nutrients that are beneficial for vision. Season the salad with olive oil so that it absorbs all the nutrients from tomatoes and leafy vegetables.

6. Potatoes.

Useful properties of potatoes for the eyes are due to beta-carotene. Like carrots, potatoes are very rich in this element, as well as vitamin A, vitamin C, fiber, magnesium and potassium, which are also essential for eye health.

7. Ostrich meat.

It may seem strange, but ostrich meat is very good for the eyes, because it is rich in zinc. Zinc plays an important role for good vision. Low levels of zinc in the retina in macular degeneration can be it is compensated by regular consumption of ostrich meat.

Ostrich meat is an excellent substitute for red meat. It contains much less fat and cholesterol than other types of red meat.
