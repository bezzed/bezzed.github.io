Nutritional errors that need to be corrected

Nutritional errors that need to be corrected

Forcing yourself or a family member to stop making nutritional mistakes can become a real household problem. In a sense, it is true that we are what we eat, and therefore proper nutrition should be one of our top priorities.

Below are some mistakes that we make in nutrition.

Meal planning.

Most people (up to 80%, according to some studies) do not plan a weekly meal. Total the result is an unbalanced diet that leads to excess weight. At for this reason, you need to have a basic knowledge of nutrition. You need to be constantly interested in the products and know what you are consuming. If there is no organization, it is easy to give preference to fast food.

In a fast-changing world, proper nutrition requires careful analysis and planning. Make reasonable decisions. shopping, carefully choosing them.

"Light food" does not always mean "healthy".

The body needs fat. Do not cling to "light" foods and do not consume these foods too often, as this will change the nutritional balance of your diet. If you want a light snack, choose light food, for example vegetables, fruits, nuts, fish, etc.

We are wrong in many ways when we believe that fat is harmful. Fat is necessary for the body to perform its functions normally. So stay away from foods labeled "0% fat content". They not only do not contain bad fats such as TRANS fats, but they also do not contain good ones! For example, monounsaturated fats and omega-3 fatty acids are very necessary for our body, and these needs must be filled.

A rare meal.

Experts recommend five meals a day. As a rule, this is a standard three-day meal - Breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as two additional meals. When we we do not eat for a long time (more than 3-4 hours), the body goes into an alarm mode. This provokes mechanisms of reserve energy storage.

What does it mean?

The body begins to conserve energy and store fat if we don't eat often enough to withstand the hours when there is no reinforcement. Therefore, it is important avoid these mechanisms in order to maintain the correct balance.

In conclusion, it is worth Recalling that if you have any questions about changing your diet or about proper nutrition in General, then you can talk to a nutritionist, who will definitely help.
